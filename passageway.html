Through the passageway

You head into the passageway, leading into complete darkness. you begin grasping at the walls on either side of you to guide your way along this cold and creepy passageway....you've fallen through a false floor into a room about the size of your body...feeling around you find a knob and turn. the knob was connected to a door that opens as you push. you are blinded by sunlight and find yourself on a cliff, with no way but down. as you look around you can see a parachute and a rope leading down the cliff...you dont know if you can trust the parachute and at the same time, you dont know if the rope will hold...
